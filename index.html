D&D Deck Draw
This app uses a deck of 12 cards to generate ability scores for your D&D character. You pull two cards at a time and add them together to get an ability score. You do this six times to get your array.

Please enter an array of 12 numbers, each number separated by a comma - ex: 4,4,4,6,6,6,6,7,7,7,9,9
